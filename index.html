Ellie Irons


I am an educator and artist with an interest in ecology. I teach drawing, interdisciplinary arts, and electronic media at City College. I also teach art to children through the Guggenheim Museum. In my own studio, I create drawings, videos and environmental sculptures that investigate human relationships to what we commonly call "nature".
